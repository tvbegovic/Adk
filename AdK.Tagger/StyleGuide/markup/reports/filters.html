Filters

General styles

  • Wrapp filters with div element that have class "report-filters"

Available filter directives

  • report-channel-filter
  • report-includes-filter
  • report-value-filter
  • report-brand-or-advertiser-filter
  • report-time-span-filter
  • report-date-range-filter
  • report-day-of-week-filter
  • report-period-filter
  • report-day-part-filter

Example:

    
      
        <div class="report-filters">
          <report-channel-filter on-init="onChannelsLoad(channels)" on-change="load()"></report-channel-filter >
          <report-includes-filter on-change="load()" on-init="onDirectivesInit()"></report-includes-filter >
          <report-value-filter on-change="load()" on-init="onDirectivesInit()"></report-value-filter >
          <report-brand-or-advertiser-filter on-init="onDirectivesInit()" on-change="load()"></report-brand-or-advertiser-filter >
          <report-time-span-filter on-init="onDirectivesInit()" on-change="load()"></report-time-span-filter >
          <report-date-range-filter on-change="load()" on-init="onDirectivesInit()"></report-date-range-filter >
          <report-day-of-week-filter on-init="onDirectivesInit()" on-change="load()"></report-day-of-week-filter >
          <report-period-filter on-init="onDirectivesInit()" on-change="load()"></report-period-filter>
          <report-day-part-filter on-init="onDirectivesInit()" on-change="load()"></report-day-part-filter>
        </div>
